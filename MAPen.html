About me

With a Master's degree in Automatic Language Processing (2024), I have worked on morphosemantics and lexical derivation through computational analysis of Russian suffixes. My background has given me expertise in derivational morphology, morphosyntactic analysis and distributional representations (word embeddings). I wish to continue this research at doctoral level, particularly in the computational modelling of word formation from a multilingual perspective.
